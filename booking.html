Got Questions

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

IS THE DISCOVERY CALL REALLY FREE?

Absolutely. The 30-minute discovery call has no cost and no obligation. It's a chance to talk through your goals and see if we're the right fit — nothing more.

WHERE DO SESSIONS TAKE PLACE?

Sessions can be held at a gym of your choice, your home, outdoors, or online via video call. Location is flexible and agreed before your first session.

HOW QUICKLY WILL I HEAR BACK?

Fabian personally reviews all booking requests and will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your session and answer any questions.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING TO MY FIRST SESSION?

Comfortable training clothes, water, and an open mind. Fabian will handle the equipment, the programming, and everything else.

CAN I CANCEL OR RESCHEDULE?

Yes — sessions can be rescheduled with at least 24 hours' notice. Fabian is flexible and understands that life happens.

DO YOU OFFER ONLINE COACHING?

Yes. Full online coaching packages are available including custom programming, nutrition plans, weekly check-ins, and video feedback on your form.
